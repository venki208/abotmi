Client Ranking

  • Here is an opportunity to showcase that all the goodwill you have built in the past
  • Here is an opportunity to showcase the loyalty that you hold with your client base
  • Here is an opportunity, what your customer thinks about you
  • Here is an opportunity to improve yourself and become better to best.

Invite your existing clients from various part of your business and ask them to rank you, rank your services, rank your skills that you possess, rank your communication, product knowledge, allow them to do it by their way.

Client ranking will enable the new clients to know more about every advisor by knowing what their existing client think about them, how they felt about them in their past dealings.

Peer Rating

To build your credit worthiness, build trust between the community to show the strength of the network and community built by you, here is an opportunity for every advisor to explore all the potential to the new client as well as to the existing client.

Send an invite to the industry peers, ask them to Rate you.

It’s a simple process, share their name, email id and the mobile number and invite them. We will do the rest, your peer will be invited to rate you by mail, by sms, if needed even we will call and follow up to rate you.

The objective is to establish the trust, build social community in understanding the advisor better, and establish a reference point, which can also lead to acquire new client relationship.

Loop

Sharing is caring

How it works

  1. You can loop any advisor from any location.
  2. All you have to do is to share name, email id, mobile number and the city of the proposed advisor; we will contact them with your reference.
  3. This advisor will be added in your loop.
  4. Every time when you refer an advisor you earn 10 points.
  5. You can redeem this points at the end of every year.
  6. On the registration of the advisor you get another 10 points.
  7. On certification of the advisor you get another 10 points.
  8. On the first transaction of the advisor you get 10 points.
  9. On verification of the advisor you get 10 more points.
  10. Every time when that advisor introduces any other advisor you get 2 points.
  11. Every time when that advisor does a transaction you get 1 point.
  12. When you get 100 points you get bonus of 5 more points.
  13. On every 100 points you reach you get an additional bonus points as surprise.

Terms and Conditions

  1. You can always share, connect, and discuss opportunities.
  2. You will get the first information on their activities.
  3. You can share your activities to them.
  4. You will be peer rated and you can rate and rank them through peer rating.

D&B Verified advisor

On your registration you can apply for D&B Verification, any registered advisor can participate in this.

The verification and the award is an independent process of D&B, it’s as per their grading criteria.

Every D& B verified advisor can use the D&B verification in all their communication materials with the unique number.

To get D&B Verified, every advisor has to pay onetime fee as per the terms and conditions of D&B.
