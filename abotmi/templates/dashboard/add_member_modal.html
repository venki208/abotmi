How to add your Client?

Add a Client

  • You can register your client on his/her behalf.
  • Key in all the necessary KYC details of your client.
  • Click “Save & Close”.
  • A confirmation mail would be sent to both you and your client.
  • Your client has to upload the required documents.
  • Once the required documents are uploaded, on confirmation and verification, you and your client would get the login credentials.
  • you can manage your client’s investments through your dashboard, with his/her consent.

Add multiple Clients

  • Adding multiple clients is similar to Add a client procedure.
  • Key in all the necessary KYC details of your client.
  • Click “Save & Add Client”.
  • Continue with the steps listed in Add a client procedure.
