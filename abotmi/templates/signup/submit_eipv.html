What is E-IPV?

E-IPV is electronic In-Person Verification as mandated for KYC compliance. During the eIPV process, you will be requested to enable video camera in your computer. Video will be captured to verify 1. your presence - make sure that your face is well covered in the video 2. Any Govt issued Identity documents. Please keep the documents handy before starting the eIPV process. Our backend team will verify the recording against the details we have got through E-IPV processes.
