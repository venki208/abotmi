What is Electronic In-Person Verification (E-IPV) ?

E-IPV is an Electronic In-Person Verification that is mandated for KYC compliance. During the E-IPV process, you will be requested to enable video camera in your computer. Video will be captured to verify:

  1. Your Identity - please make sure your entire face is in the screen.
  2. Any government issued identity documents.

Please keep the documents handy before starting the E-IPV process. Our back-end team will verify the recording against the details we have got through E-IPV processes.
