Benefits of being a Certified REIA

As a REIA, you will be able to tap into a bouquet of support service to boost your Knowledge and expand your Client Base.

Knowledge

  • Ongoing international standard training, both Online and Offline
  • Research reports and in-depth market intelligence
  • Discussion forum with fellow Advisors
  • Webinars and Seminars
  • Mentoring and guidance from Experts
Training
How to provide Advisory;
How to analyse Opportunities;
How to tap Technology to market every Opportunity.
Global Market Exposure
Specially arranged global interactions through Webinars, field trips to other markets, events, expo to know and understand how the Global Market works.
Product info kit / Training
A detailed product related “MIS” and Weekly Webinars to help the REIA to handle clients.
Knowledge Platform - ICORE
An exclusive platform of Blog, Forum that will help the REIA to get updated market info, client expectations and media news.

Opportunities and Reach

Connect with Global Investors

  • Investors Lead:Potential Investors, who have expressed willingness to Invest in Opportunities will be identified and shared.
  • Opportunities-Product:Exclusive Investments Opportunities will be created and shared only to REIAs.
  • Technology-Support: Dynamic and user friendly technology platform that will help in marketing, CRM, manage operations.
  • Networking Events: Special invite-only events for registered REIAs to share discuss and grow.
  • Visual Merchandising:Exclusively created merchandise that will help the “REIA” to stand out of the crowd and communicate that we are Professionals.
  • Brand Identity: A strong brand identity will help to establish Trust, Security, Transparency and Professionalism.
